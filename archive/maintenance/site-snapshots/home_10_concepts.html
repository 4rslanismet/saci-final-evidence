Öneri 9 — Mimari odaklı giriş

Telemetry, intelligence and evidence as a graph

SACI connects assets, log sources, Wazuh rules, detection controls, MITRE techniques, CTI objects and score metrics into a single evidence graph.

Telemetry layer: Windows Security, Sysmon, PowerShell, Linux logs and pfSense syslog provide the observed signal surface.

Detection layer: Wazuh rules and controls transform telemetry into detectable scenario evidence.

Threat intelligence layer: MISP enrichment links IOC evidence to alert conversion and MITRE context.

Scoring layer: CWLC, CAC, MDC, CTIC and TF combine into the final SACI visibility score.
