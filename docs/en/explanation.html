Explanation report

This page converts deterministic SACI metrics, graph relations, reason codes and scenario states into long-form operational explanations through a policy-guided reporting layer.

Method boundary: the explanation layer does not calculate, modify or override the SACI score. It interprets deterministic outputs. An optional LLM may only rephrase the structured report produced by the policy.

Policy-guided explanation layer

The reporting policy defines what can be stated, which evidence must support it and how scope limitations must be communicated.

Optional LLM

04

Every statement remains traceable to metrics, relations or reason codes.
